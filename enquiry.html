What courses do you offer? expand_more
We offer a wide range of courses including MS-CIT, Tally Prime, Web Development, Graphic Design, Python, Advanced Programming, and Digital Marketing.
Where is the campus located? expand_more
We are located at B-9, next to Cardinal School, Subhash Nagar, Bandra East, Mumbai. It's a quick 2-minute walk from Bandra Colony metro station!
How do I enroll in a course? expand_more
You can enroll by visiting our Bandra East campus in person, or by filling out the online Enquiry form. Our staff will guide you through the process.
Are your classrooms air-conditioned? expand_more
Yes, we provide fully air-conditioned modern computer labs to ensure a comfortable and productive learning environment for all students.
Do you provide certificates? expand_more
Absolutely! Upon successful completion of your course and relevant exams, you will receive an industry-recognized certificate (e.g., from MKCL for MS-CIT).
